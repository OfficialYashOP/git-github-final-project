# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, gender identity, sexual identity, race, or religion.

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful toward different viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Using sexualized or derogatory language
- Making personal attacks
- Harassing behavior of any kind
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards and may remove comments, commits, or contributors that violate the Code.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.  
All complaints will be reviewed fairly and confidentially.
